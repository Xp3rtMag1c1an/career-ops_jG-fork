**Verdict:** Level match is strong. This is a "player" not "player-coach" role — someone who executes at high leverage personally. Josh's solo founder experience is an asset here, not a gap.

1. **Lead with Avenue Alive scale:** "I authored the playbook that 400 reps used across 40 markets. That's not just writing docs — it's understanding what scales and what doesn't."
2. **Bridge2Rwanda as ambiguity proof:** "I was dropped into Kigali with no playbook, no precedent. I shipped curriculum that's still in use 15 years later. That's what I do — walk into the unknown, find the architecture, deliver."
3. **writenode as speed proof:** "I took an idea to a production Chrome extension in months — solo, no engineering background. When I say 'days not weeks,' I mean it."
4. **Findr competitive intel:** "My competitive analysis for Findr directly shaped their product roadmap. I don't do analysis for analysis's sake — I do it to make decisions."

### "If Downleveled" Plan

Not likely given role structure, but if they frame it as "Senior Associate" or similar:
- Accept if total comp is $130K+ and there's a clear path to Lead within 6 months
- Ask for documented promotion criteria at offer stage
- The role's autonomy level matters more than the title
